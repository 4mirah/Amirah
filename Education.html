How about my education?

I started attending school at Nur Furqan Kindergarten since I was 5 years old. This kindergarten is close to my house. Usually, I go to kindergarten with my father at 8am and come home with my aunt at 12.30pm. My aunt is a teacher at the kindergarten so she will help take me back home.
When I was 7 years old, I went to school at Ibn Khaldun Primary School. This school is also close to my house. This school is not as big as other schools but I really enjoyed studying there. I have many good friends and the teacher is also good. For everyone's knowledge, I went to high school at the same school. This is because the primary and secondary schools are combined in one place. I feel comfortable studying in this school.
After finishing school, I continued my studies at UiTM Machang, Kelantan in the Information Management course. I studied for 2 and a half years which is for 5 semesters. For me this course is not very difficult because I only need to read a lot and there is no maths subject in this course.
