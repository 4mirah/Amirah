What experiences have I had throughout this life?

As soon as I finished school and finished my SPM exam, I looked for a side job close to my home. Alhamdulillah, I got 2 side jobs before entering university. First, I got a job as an assistant school librarian at my old elementary school and second, I got a job as a restaurant waiter. I worked as a librarian's assistant for 4 months and as a waiter for 4 months. I got a lot of experience while working. While being an assistant librarian, I got to know how a librarian works. I have to arrange the books, make sure all the books are arranged according to the correct shelves, make sure the library is in a clean condition, control the students while in the library, fill the book data in the computer and much more. Working as a bookkeeper's assistant is not very tiring. I enjoy my work. While I was working as a waiter, I got a different experience from the assistant librarian. This job as a waiter is very tiring. I have to serve customers, serve them food, take their orders and much more. I work from 7.30 am to 3.30 pm. Beside of that, I have join some association in university. Some association thhat I have join is Jawatankuasa Perwakilan Kolej Tun Hussein Onn (JPKTHO), Association of Information Scientist (AIS) and Persatuan Silat Cekak, UiTM Cawangan Kelantan (SCPH). I got a lot of experience through the association I join.
